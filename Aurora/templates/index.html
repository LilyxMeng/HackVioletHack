Hey There!

Welcome to Aurora, a platform for women by women devoted to providing resources that will not only inform but empower and connect women across the globe.

About Us

Our team created this website with the goal of supporting women. We decided to create Aurora by incorprating HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap for our front-end and Python and Flask for our back-end. Our design is aimed for to be easy on the eyes, aestheticly pleasing as well as elements to ease navigation.

Mission

Our Mission is to provide resources to women. Many women often have little knowledge of the helpful resources that they can use. We decided to help out our community of amazing women and place resources on Aurora. On here you can find job oppurtunites, find events around you, connect with other spectacular women, and find handy hotlines.
